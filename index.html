About Us:

Picky Eaters is an affordable restaurant that offers a variety of dishes, including pizza, salads, pasta, burgers, seafood and desserts. We also offer 3 different special meals that change every week and can be found on the top of our home page. Customers can also order supplements, such as fries or a drink, which are placed at the bottom of our menu section. We additionally offer an option to customize your own meal for some dishes, which gives you full control to pick what is in your food. There is both an option to order our food online, or simply visit our restaurant which is located as shown on the map below.
